# Account Deletion Instructions

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## How to Delete Your Adify Account

You can delete your Adify account at any time. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

### Methods to Request Account Deletion

#### Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)
1. Send an email to: **admin@adrevtechnologies.com**
2. Use subject line: **"Delete My Account"**
3. Include your registered email address
4. We will process your request within **30 days**

#### Method 2: Through App Settings (When Available)
1. Open the Adify app
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Scroll to **Account Management**
4. Tap **Delete My Account**
5. Confirm deletion
6. Your account will be deleted within **30 days**
