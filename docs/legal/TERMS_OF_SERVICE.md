## 6. Regional Rate Multipliers

### 6.1 Purpose and Rationale

Regional Rate Multipliers exist to reflect the reality that advertising revenue varies significantly across different geographic markets. Advertisers pay different rates for impressions and clicks based on the purchasing power, market size, and competitive landscape of each region. To maintain platform sustainability while providing fair compensation, Adify applies multipliers that correlate with the actual revenue generated from each country.

### 6.2 Multiplier Ranges

Regional Rate Multipliers range from **0.8x to 1.2x** and are applied to base AdCoin earning rates:

- **Tier 1 Markets (1.2x multiplier):** Countries with highest advertising CPM rates, typically including United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Germany, and other high-value advertising markets
- **Tier 2 Markets (1.0x multiplier):** Countries with moderate advertising CPM rates, including many European, Asian, and South American markets
- **Tier 3 Markets (0.8x multiplier):** Countries with lower advertising CPM rates, often due to smaller market size or lower advertiser competition

### 6.3 Multiplier Determination

Your Regional Rate Multiplier is determined by:

- **IP Address Location:** Primary method for determining geographic location
- **Account Registration Country:** Country specified during account creation
- **Payment Method Location:** Geographic location associated with your PayPal account
- **Consistency Verification:** Our systems verify that these indicators are consistent to prevent location manipulation

### 6.4 Prohibited Location Manipulation

The following activities related to location manipulation are strictly prohibited:

- Use of VPN services to misrepresent geographic location
- Use of proxy servers to access higher-multiplier regions
- GPS spoofing or location falsification
- Account registration using false address information
- Any other attempt to circumvent Regional Rate Multipliers

Violation of these prohibitions will result in immediate account termination and forfeiture of all balances.

### 6.5 Multiplier Changes

Regional Rate Multipliers may be updated periodically to reflect:

- Changes in advertising market conditions
- Currency exchange rate fluctuations
- New market data from AdMob
- Platform economic adjustments

Users will be notified of material changes to their Regional Rate Multiplier through in-app notifications and email.

### 6.6 Transparency

Your current Regional Rate Multiplier is displayed in your account dashboard. We provide this transparency to ensure users understand how their earnings are calculated.
