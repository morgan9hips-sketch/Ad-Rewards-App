## 3. Minimum Withdrawal Threshold

### 3.1 Threshold Amount

**Minimum Requirement: 15,000 AdCoins Cash Balance**

You must accumulate at least 15,000 AdCoins converted to Cash Balance before you can request a withdrawal.

**Rationale:**
- **Transaction Costs:** Minimizes payment processing costs relative to payout amounts
- **Fraud Prevention:** Reduces incentive for small-scale fraud attempts
- **Platform Sustainability:** Ensures economic viability of the platform
- **User Benefit:** Provides meaningful payout amounts worth the processing time

### 3.2 Cash Balance Calculation

**AdCoins vs. Cash Balance:**
- **AdCoins:** Virtual currency earned through Platform activities
- **Cash Balance:** Monetary value of AdCoins calculated using current conversion rates and Regional Rate Multipliers
- **Threshold Applies To:** Cash Balance equivalent to 15,000 AdCoins

**Example:**
- Base conversion: 1,000 AdCoins = $1.00 USD
- Regional Rate Multiplier: 1.0x (Tier 2 country)
- 15,000 AdCoins = $15.00 USD Cash Balance (meets threshold)

**Regional Variations:**
Due to Regional Rate Multipliers (0.8x to 1.2x), the actual cash value of 15,000 AdCoins varies by country:
- **Tier 1 (1.2x multiplier):** 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $18.00 USD
- **Tier 2 (1.0x multiplier):** 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $15.00 USD
- **Tier 3 (0.8x multiplier):** 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $12.00 USD

**Note:** Exact values depend on current conversion rates, which may fluctuate.

### 3.3 Below-Threshold Balances

If your Cash Balance is below 15,000 AdCoins:

- You cannot request a withdrawal
- Continue earning AdCoins through Platform activities
- Balance is retained safely in your account
- No expiration if you remain active (see Balance Expiry policy in Terms of Service)

### 3.4 No Minimum for Account Closure

**Exception:** If you choose to close your account entirely, you forfeit any balance below the minimum withdrawal threshold. We do not make exceptions for below-threshold withdrawals, even for account closures.

**Recommendation:** Reach the minimum threshold before closing your account to avoid forfeiting earned funds.

## 5. Withdrawal Processing Time

### 5.1 Total Processing Time: 7-14 Business Days

From the time you submit a withdrawal request to the time funds appear in your PayPal account, the process typically takes **7-14 business days**.

**Breakdown:**

**Phase 1: Review Period (3-5 business days)**
- Fraud detection analysis
- Account verification checks
- Activity pattern review
- Compliance checks

**Phase 2: Processing Period (2-5 business days)**
- Payment preparation
- Batch processing (withdrawals are often batched for efficiency)
- Finalization and approval

**Phase 3: Transfer Period (2-4 business days)**
- Transfer initiated to PayPal
- PayPal processes incoming payment
- Funds appear in your PayPal balance

### 5.2 Business Days Defined

**Business Days:**
- Monday through Friday
- Excludes weekends (Saturday and Sunday)
- Excludes South African public holidays
- Excludes major international holidays (when applicable)

**Example:** Withdrawal requested on Friday may not begin processing until the following Monday.

### 5.3 Factors Affecting Processing Time

**Faster Processing (7-10 days):**
- Established account with withdrawal history

- Low-risk account profile

**Slower Processing (10-14 days or longer):**
- First withdrawal (requires thorough verification)
- Large withdrawal amounts (additional scrutiny)
- Suspicious activity patterns detected
- Additional verification requested
- High-volume periods (many users requesting withdrawals)
- Holidays or weekends extending timeframe

### 5.4 Priority Processing

- Elite subscribers receive priority support, which may expedite resolution of withdrawal issues

- Elite does not bypass mandatory security reviews

**No Expedited Service:**

### 5.5 Delays Beyond Our Control

Some delays are beyond our control:

**PayPal Processing:**
- PayPal may take additional time to credit your account

- PayPal account issues (limited account, verification required)

**Banking Delays:**
- If you withdraw from PayPal to your bank, additional 2-5 business days apply

**International Transfers:**
- Currency conversion may add processing time

### 5.6 Tracking Your Withdrawal

## 8. Regional Rate Multipliers

### 8.1 Overview

**Regional Rate Multipliers** adjust AdCoin earning rates based on the advertising revenue generated from your country. Multipliers range from **0.8x to 1.2x** and directly impact the cash value of your AdCoins.

**Purpose:**
- Reflect actual advertising revenue by country (CPM rates vary globally)
- Ensure platform sustainability
- Provide fair compensation proportional to revenue generated

### 8.2 Multiplier Tiers

**Tier 1: High-Value Markets (1.2x multiplier)**
- Countries: United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Germany, Switzerland, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Netherlands, etc.
- Characteristics: High advertising CPM rates, strong purchasing power, competitive ad markets
- Effect: 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $18.00 USD Cash Balance

**Tier 2: Moderate-Value Markets (1.0x multiplier)**
- Countries: Most European countries, Japan, South Korea, Singapore, many South American countries, South Africa, etc.
- Characteristics: Moderate advertising CPM rates, established digital ad markets
- Effect: 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $15.00 USD Cash Balance

**Tier 3: Developing Markets (0.8x multiplier)**
- Countries: Many Asian, African, Eastern European, and Latin American countries
- Characteristics: Lower advertising CPM rates, smaller ad markets, developing economies
- Effect: 15,000 AdCoins ‚âà $12.00 USD Cash Balance

**Note:** Specific country classifications may change over time based on market data.

### 8.3 How Multipliers Are Determined

**Data Sources:**
- Google AdMob revenue data by country
- Historical CPM (cost per thousand impressions) rates
- Currency exchange rates
- Market size and competition

**Updates:**
- Multipliers reviewed quarterly
- Adjusted based on actual advertising revenue trends
- Users notified of material changes (see Terms of Service)

### 8.4 Location Verification

**Your Multiplier Is Based On:**
1. **Account Registration Country:** Country selected during signup
2. **IP Address:** Current and historical IP addresses
3. **PayPal Account Location:** Country of your PayPal account

**Consistency Required:**
All three indicators should align. Mismatched locations trigger additional verification or withdrawal denial.

**Examples of Mismatches:**
- Account registered in US, but IP address consistently shows India
- PayPal account in UK, but account registered in Nigeria
- IP addresses from multiple countries (possible VPN use)

### 8.5 Prohibited Multiplier Manipulation

**Strictly Forbidden:**
- **VPN Use:** Using VPNs to appear in higher-multiplier countries
- **Proxy Servers:** Routing traffic through proxies to fake location
- **GPS Spoofing:** Falsifying GPS location (if location permissions were granted)
- **False Registration:** Registering with false country information

**Detection:**
- Our fraud detection systems identify VPN/proxy usage
- Device fingerprinting detects location inconsistencies
- AdMob data cross-referenced with account location

**Consequences:**
- Immediate account termination
- Forfeiture of all AdCoins and Cash Balance
- Ban on creating future accounts
- No warnings or appeals for blatant manipulation

### 8.6 Traveling and Moving

**Legitimate Location Changes:**

**Traveling:**
- Temporary travel to other countries is acceptable
- Short-term IP address changes from travel do not affect multiplier
- Your multiplier remains based on account registration country
- Extended travel (several months) may trigger verification

**Permanent Relocation:**
- If you move to a different country, contact support@adrevtechnologies.co.za
- Provide proof of relocation (new address verification)
- Account location and multiplier will be updated after verification
- PayPal account should also be updated to new country

### 8.7 Transparency

**Viewing Your Multiplier:**
- Your current Regional Rate Multiplier is displayed in Account Dashboard ‚Üí Earnings Settings
- Conversion calculations show how multiplier affects your Cash Balance
- Transparent display ensures you understand your earning potential
