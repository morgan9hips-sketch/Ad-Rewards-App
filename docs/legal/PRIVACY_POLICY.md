## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR/POPIA)

Under GDPR and POPIA, we must have a lawful basis for processing your personal information. We rely on the following legal bases:

### 4.1 Contract Performance (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b), POPIA Section 11(1)(a))

Processing is necessary to fulfill our contract with you (the Terms of Service), including:

- Creating and managing your account
- Providing Platform services
- Processing earnings and withdrawals
- Delivering customer support
- Enforcing Terms of Service

**Your Rights:** If you request deletion of data processed for contract performance, we may be unable to continue providing services.

### 4.2 Legal Obligation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c), POPIA Section 11(1)(b))

Processing is necessary to comply with legal obligations, including:

- Tax reporting and compliance
- Anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
- Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements
- Record-keeping obligations
- Responding to lawful government requests
- Protecting against fraud and financial crimes

**Data Retention:** Data processed for legal compliance must be retained for legally mandated periods (typically 7 years for financial records).

### 4.3 Legitimate Interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f), POPIA Section 11(1)(f))

Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests or those of third parties, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights:

**Our Legitimate Interests:**
- Fraud prevention and platform security
- Platform improvement and optimization
- Business analytics and planning
- Network and information security
- Enforcing legal rights
- Preventing abuse and Terms violations

**Balancing Test:** We have conducted balancing tests to ensure our legitimate interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms. You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

### 4.4 Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a), POPIA Section 11(1)(a))

For certain processing activities, we rely on your explicit consent:

- Marketing communications (promotional emails)
- Optional data collection (profile pictures, bio)
- Cookies and tracking technologies (where required)
- Social media integration (if applicable)
- Data sharing with specific third parties (where not necessary for service provision)

**Withdrawal of Consent:** You may withdraw consent at any time through account settings. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

### 4.5 Vital Interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(d), POPIA Section 11(1)(d))

In rare circumstances, processing may be necessary to protect vital interests:

- Emergency situations requiring immediate action
- Life-threatening circumstances
- Protection of health and safety

### 4.6 Public Interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(e), POPIA Section 11(1)(c))

We do not generally process data for public interest tasks, but this basis may apply in exceptional circumstances involving regulatory cooperation or public health emergencies.

- Update outdated information

**How to Exercise:**
- Update most information directly in account settings
- Email privacy@adrevtechnologies.co.za for data you cannot modify yourself

**Response Time:** Corrections made within 5 business days, or 30 days for complex requests

### 7.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (GDPR Art. 17, POPIA Section 23)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data when:

- Data is no longer necessary for its original purpose
- You withdraw consent (for consent-based processing)
- You object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Data has been unlawfully processed
- Legal obligation requires deletion
- Data was collected from a child without proper consent

**Limitations:** We may refuse deletion if processing is necessary for:
- Legal obligations
- Legal claims
- Public interest tasks
- Archiving and research purposes

**How to Exercise:**
- Close your account through account settings (triggers 30-day deletion process)
- Email privacy@adrevtechnologies.co.za with "Data Deletion Request"

**What Happens:**
- Personal data deleted within 30 days
- Transaction data retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Fraud prevention data retained indefinitely
- You will receive confirmation of deletion

### 7.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (GDPR Art. 18, POPIA Section 25)

You have the right to request that we limit how we use your data when:

- You contest the accuracy of the data (during verification)
- Processing is unlawful, but you don't want deletion
- We no longer need the data, but you need it for legal claims

**Effect:** We will store the data but not actively process it (except with your consent or for legal purposes)

**How to Exercise:** Email privacy@adrevtechnologies.co.za with "Restriction Request"

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (GDPR Art. 20)

You have the right to:

- Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (e.g., JSON, CSV)

**Scope:** Applies only to data you provided and that we process based on consent or contract

**Excludes:** Data derived or inferred by us, data processed for legitimate interests

## 9. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

### 9.1 Encryption

**In Transit:**
- **TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security):** All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using the latest TLS 1.3 protocol
- **HTTPS Everywhere:** All web communications use HTTPS with strong cipher suites
- **Certificate Pinning:** Mobile app uses certificate pinning to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks

**At Rest:**
- **AES-256 Encryption:** All sensitive data stored in databases is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **Database Encryption:** Supabase database uses encryption at rest
- **Encrypted Backups:** All data backups are encrypted

**Password Security:**
- **Bcrypt Hashing:** Passwords are hashed using Bcrypt with high work factors
- **No Plain Text:** We never store passwords in plain text
- **Salted Hashes:** Each password hash uses a unique salt
- **No Password Recovery:** We cannot retrieve your password (only reset it)

### 9.2 Authentication and Access Control

- Industry-standard authentication protocol

- Short-lived access tokens
- Refresh token rotation
